Programs

SWDAA For Education

SWDAA For Education is an initiative created by the SWDAA Board of Directors and the Finance Committee as a way to increase the recognition of those alumni members who are serving as educators in our District. We recognize that being an educator has an impact on the future of our society that often goes unnoticed. We are also immensely proud of the alumni members of Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma who have dedicated their lives to the enrichment of others through education.




Striving for Greater Professionals

Striving For Greater Professionals (SFGP) is a programming initiative from SWDAA designed to promote and encourage professional development for our alumni at all stages in their career paths. Whether you’re just starting out on the job hunt or navigating your current career trajectory, you should never feel alone. Our members are our greatest resource for growth, and we want to make it as easy as possible for you to create meaningful personal and professional networks within our organization. By offering specially crafted workshops, webinars, networking events, and resources, the Striving for Greater Professionals Program enables our members to continue to grow and thrive in their professional lives.


SWDAA Couch Surfing

Couchsurfing is defined as: “to stay temporarily in a series of other people's homes, typically making use of improvised sleeping arrangements.” Many brothers and sisters across the district are willing to open up their home to traveling members who may need a place to stay for a night. We now have one place where people can look at to find places they may be able to stay, as well as offer up their home to other traveling brothers and sisters. SWDAA Programs Committee

If you have ideas, opinions, questions, challenges, desire, innovation, gumption, dedication, funness or none of the above and you’re interested in improving the programs of the SWDAA, then grab a seat at the table and join the SWDAA Programs Committee. You can join at any time simply by emailing swdaaprograms@gmail.com informing us of your interest.
The committee will meet regularly to plan, executive, and improve the programs of the Southwest District Alumni Association. Members will be expected to take on at least one program to help develop and carry out at some point during the year.
