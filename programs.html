What do we do?

Our programs aim to address the four main purposes of the SWDAA.

  1. To foster a spirit of personal and professional growth within the Alumni members of the Southwest District
  2. To facilitate social functions and networking opportunities to the members of the Southwest District Alumni Association
  3. To provide opportunities for service which are consistent with the mutual purposes of Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma
  4. To promote and assist the activities of the Southwest District and to foster a personal connection between Active members and the Southwest District Alumni Association

SWDAA CouchSurfing

Couchsurfing is defined as: “to stay temporarily in a series of other people's homes, typically making use of improvised sleeping arrangements.” Many brothers and sisters across the district are willing to open up their home to traveling members who may need a place to stay for a night. We now have one place where people can look at to find places they may be able to stay, as well as offer up their home to other traveling brothers and sisters. SWDAA Programs Committee

If you have ideas, opinions, questions, challenges, desire, innovation, gumption, dedication, funness or none of the above and you’re interested in improving the programs of the SWDAA, then grab a seat at the table and join the SWDAA Programs Committee. You can join at any time simply by emailing swdaaprograms@gmail.com informing us of your interest. The committee will meet regularly to plan, executive, and improve the programs of the Southwest District Alumni Association. Members will be expected to take on at least one program to help develop and carry out at some point during the year.
