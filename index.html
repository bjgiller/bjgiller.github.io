OFFICIAL WEBSITE FOR THE ALUMNI OF THE SOUTHWEST DISTRICT OF Kappa Kappa Psi & Tau Beta Sigma

The Southwest District Alumni Association provides collaboration and social opportunities for Alumni, Honorary, and Life Members of the Southwest District of Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma. Alumni serving programs throughout New Mexico, Arkansas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, and Texas!

Why Join SWDAA?

Dale Grimm, Theta Theta (ΤΒΣ, Life)

“I joined SWDAA so that I am still connected with Tau Beta Sigma and that I can help make an impact to help Tau Beta Sigma and Kappa Kappa Psi grow. SWDAA is a great organization for those who have just graduated from college as well as life members and still want to be apart of Tau Beta Sigma and Kappa Kappa Psi even after graduation!”

Yslette Arispe, Iota Tau (ΤΒΣ, Alumni)

“I joined SWDAA to once again make lifelong friendships with other alumni sisters from all over the district and to be able to continue my love for an organization that not only accepted me with a disability but allowed me to be myself and make friends that I never thought possible.”

Katelyn Huckaby, Alpha Psi (ΚΚΨ, Life)

"You'll spend more time as an alumnus than you will as an active member and SWDAA is a place to stay involved with the district, keep in contact with old friends, and make new ones. You can connect with people in your field and in different fields all while being connected through the bonds of music."

Christopher Giles, KKPsi AABOD, Epsilon Chi (ΚΚΨ, Life)

“One reason I joined SWADAA because the continuation of my oath to the bond continues throughout SWDAA. We show up and show out to support and foster college and university bands on great alumni level. AEA”.
