Board of Directors

Brianna Mitchell

Chair (She/Her)

Brianna Mitchell is a Life Member of the Alpha Omicron chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Sam Houston State University in Huntsville, Texas. She was initiated in the fall of 2009. While an active member she served in several roles including Music Chair, Treasurer, Vice President, and President. As a member of the Bearkat Marching Band, she served as a Mellophone Visual Technician and as a Student Assistant. Brianna graduated in 2014 with her Bachelor of Music in Music Education.

After her graduation, she was an Assistant Band Director for three years at Tennyson Middle School in Waco, Texas. She now lives in Bay City, Texas and is the Head Band Director for Sweeny Junior High and assists at Sweeny High School as well as serving as the colorguard sponsor.

As a person who likes to stay busy, Brianna also serves Tau Beta Sigma as a Chapter Visitation Assistant, plays the French horn in the Brazosport Symphony Orchestra, serves on the board of the BSO, and is a Gold Star member of TBSAA. When she has free time, she likes to hang out with her boyfriend, Scott, play video games, and cuddle with her dogs Remus and Seamus (who you can follow on Instagram @twomusketeers_adventures)

Contact: swdaachair@gmail.com

Evelyn Jones

Membership Director (She/Her)

Evelyn (Eve) Jones was initiated to the Beta Nu Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Arkansas Tech University in the spring of 2015. She first graduated in 2012 with a Bachelor’s of Science in Professional Studies. After returning to Arkansas Tech in 2013, she graduated with her second Bachelor's of Science degree in Early Childhood Education in 2017.

As an active member, she served 1 year as Historian and was a part of several committees. She currently works in Arkansas at Paris Elementary School as a First Grade teacher. While in college, she was also a member of Sigma Alpha Iota serving on the executive board as Vice President of Membership (2 years) and Sergeant of Arms.

She is married with 3 beautiful children and 4 fur babies. She has been married for almost 10 years but they have been together for 15 years. Her children are Alex age 6, Arianna age 4, and Julianna age 2. Of her fur babies, she has 2 cats named Poncho and Simba and 2 dogs named McGee and Sheara. She loves being with her family/friends and working with children. Miniature, long-haired weenie dogs are her favorite breed of dog. She is a huge Disney and Star Wars fan. She also collects shot glasses of places and states that she has been to. In her free time, she enjoys reading, playing video games, watching tv, 4-wheeling, making music, playing softball and basketball with her kids and archery. Contact Eve at swdaamembership@gmail.com. You can also link up with her on Facebook, Instagram or Snapchat.

Contact: swdaamembership@gmail.com

Natalia Harrison

Programs Director (She/Her)

Natalia is a Life member of the Alpha Omicron chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Sam Houston State University. She became a member in the Spring of 2015. During her time as an Active sister in AO, Natalia served as the Recording Secretary, President, and two terms as the Parliamentarian. Along with her chapter duties, Natalia served the Southwest District as the District Secretary/Treasure for one term and District President for two terms.

Natalia currently lives in Conroe, TX with her husband, Jamie Harrison and their dog, Phoebe. She works as an elementary music teacher and in her free time, she enjoys playing disc golf, playing board games, and cooking!

Dionicio Cardenas

Communications Director (He/Him)

Dionicio Cardenas was initiated into the Alpha Psi chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi at West Texas A&M University in the spring of 2013. As an active member, Dionicio served as Alpha Psi’s Ritual Officer, Historian, and Vice President of Membership. He also served as the SWD Member-at-Large for the 2017-2018 term and as the Chair of the National Communications Team of Kappa Kappa Psi for the 2019 - 2021 term. He graduated in May of 2020 with a Bachelor’s of Music. On November 21, 2020, Dionicio became a Honorary member of the Xi Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma. He currently lives in Rio Bravo, Texas where he's a freelance music educator. In his free time, he enjoys playing Pokémon GO, collecting Funko Pops, watching Marvel movies and shows, and playing with his friend, Gus the Cat.

KKPsi Member at Large (He/Him)

Sara Remoy

TBS Member at Large (She/Her)

Sara Remoy was initiated into the Psi chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at the University of Arkansas in Fall 2012. During her time as an active member, Sara served as the Vice President of Membership! Education during the 2014-2015 school year and was selected as the 2016 Southwest District Keynote Speaker. Sara graduated from the University of Arkansas in December 2016 with a Bachelor of Music degree in Instrumental Education. Sara is currently pursuing a Doctor of Musical Arts in Bassoon Performance at the University of Iowa. Sara has been a member of SWDAA since finishing her bachelor’s degree and has previously served on the board as Membership Director from 2018-2019. During her free time, Sara enjoys watching TV, reading, and crocheting. Sara currently lives in Iowa City with her two cats, Kami and Kara.
