Board of Directors

Brianna Mitchell

Chairperson (She/Her)

8 Years in SWDAA

Brianna Mitchell is a Life Member of the Alpha Omicron chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Sam Houston State University in Huntsville, Texas. She was initiated in the fall of 2009. While an active member she served in several roles including Music Chair, Treasurer, Vice President, and President. As a member of the Bearkat Marching Band, she served as a Mellophone Visual Technician and as a Student Assistant. Brianna graduated in 2014 with her Bachelor of Music in Music Education.

After her graduation, she was an Assistant Band Director for three years at Tennyson Middle School in Waco, Texas. She now lives in Bay City, Texas and is the Head Band Director for Sweeny Junior High and assists at Sweeny High School as well as serving as the colorguard sponsor.

As a person who likes to stay busy, Brianna also serves Tau Beta Sigma as a Chapter Visitation Assistant, plays the French horn in the Brazosport Symphony Orchestra, serves on the board of the BSO as Parliamentarian, and is a Gold Star member of TBSAA. When she has free time, she likes to hang out with her fiancé, Scott, play video games, and cuddle with her chiweenie, Remus.

Contact: swdaachair@gmail.com

Jennifer D. Villarreal

Membership Director (She/Her)

2 Years in SWDAA

Jennifer D. Villarreal is a Life member of the Beta Zeta chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Stephen F. Austin State University. She became a member in the Spring of 2008 and served as Service and Special Projects Chair, Secretary/Treasurer, and President while an active member.

Jennifer is in her 10th year of teaching and currently resides in Rio Grande City, Texas. She serves as the head band director at Roma Middle School in Roma, Texas and oversees the flute studio in Roma ISD, grades 6 - 12. In her free time, she likes to hike, take photographs, and spend time with her family and dog Winston Porter.

Contact: swdaamembership@gmail.com

Natalia Harrison

Programs Director (She/Her)

3 Years in SWDAA

Natalia Harrison is a Life Member of the Alpha Omicron chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Sam Houston State University in Huntsville, TX. While at SHSU, Natalia served in multiple officer positions for her chapter. In 2016, she was elected to the position of Southwest Secretary/Treasurer and went on to serve two terms as the Southwest District President from 2017- 2019.

After graduating, she became a member of Tau Beta Sigma Alumni Association and the Southwest District Alumni Association where she has served as the Education Coordinator and Programs Director for SWDAA. She also serves as a member of the National Leadership team as a Chapter Visitation Assistant. In addition, she currently serves on the TBS History and Archives Committee and TBSAA Programs Committee.

Natalia now lives in Conroe, TX where she works as an elementary music teacher. In her free time, she likes to play disc golf with her husband Jamie, cuddle her dog Phoebe, and go thrift shopping around the Houston area.

Contact: swdaaprograms@gmail.com

Dionicio Cardenas

Communications Director (He/Him)

4 Years in SWDDA

Dionicio “Dino” Cardenas is Life Member of the Alpha Psi chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi at West Texas A&M University in Canyon, Texas. He was initiated on April 14, 2013. While in the Alpha Psi chapter he served as Historian, Ritual Officer, and Vice President for Membership. Dionicio also served as the Southwest District Member-at-Large for one term (17-18) where he revamped the district's social media platforms and created the Historians Facebook group. For the University program, he served as the Historian and toured with the Symphonic Band for seven seasons as their Social Media outreach member. Dionicio graduated in the Spring of 2019 with his Bachelor’s of Music degree.

After graduation, Dionicio has served as the Chair of the National Communications Team of Kappa Kappa Psi, the National Programs Committee of Kappa Kappa Psi, and the National Communications Committee of Tau Beta Sigma. Recently the Southwest District Alumni Association named him the 2022 Southwest District Kappa Kappa Psi Alumni of the Year. He holds honorary memberships from the Xi Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at West Texas A&M University and from the Southwest District of Kappa Kappa Psi. Dionicio currently lives in Laredo, Texas where he is a freelance music educator. In his free time, he enjoys playing Pokemon GO, collecting Funko Pops, watching Marvel movies and shows, and taking care of his emotional support animal, Gus the Cat.

Contact: swdjointalumni@gmail.com

Chelsea Zuniga

TBS Member at Large (She/Her)

2 Years in SWDDA

Chelsea is a Life Member of the Alpha Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Oklahoma State University. She was initiated during the spring 2016 semester her freshman year. During her time as an active, she served as Treasurer for one semester and President for five semesters. She was also very involved in OSU’s NAfME chapter, serving as Treasurer for one year and President for one year. She was initiated into the Iota Alpha Chapter of Sigma Alpha Iota in fall 2017 and served one year as Treasurer as well.

Chelsea graduated from OSU in fall 2019 with a Bachelor of Music Education in Instrumental Music Education. During her time at OSU, she was an active member of the Cowboy Marching Band, Wind Ensemble, Symphonic Band, Concert Band, and Symphony Orchestra. She also served the Cowboy Marching Band as a section leader for three seasons.

Chelsea currently resides in Oklahoma where she teaches band to grades 6-12, and is planning to return to school online in summer 2022 for her Master of Music in Music Education at the University of Central Oklahoma. Chelsea has two beautiful cats, Cricket and Junior, and over 75 plants of various sizes that she cares for.
