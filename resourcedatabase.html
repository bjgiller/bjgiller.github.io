Striving For Greater Professionals

Striving for Greater Professionals (SFGP) is a programming initiative from SWDAA designed to promote and encourage professional development for our alumni at all stages of their career path. Whether you’re just starting out on the job hunt or navigating your current career trajectory, you should never feel alone. Our members are our greatest resource for growth, and we want to make it as easy as possible for you to create meaningful personal and professional networks within our organization.
