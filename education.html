SWDAA For Education

SWDAA For Education is an initiative created by the SWDAA Board of Directors and the Finance Committee as a way to increase the recognition of those alumni members who are serving as educators in our District. We recognize that being an educator has an impact on the future of our society that often goes unnoticed. We are also immensely proud of the alumni members of Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma who have dedicated their lives to the enrichment of others through education.

Meet our Fall 2021 SWDAA for Education recipient, Alyssa Ramstad. Alyssa is an Alumna of the Eta Pi Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at Northwestern State University of Louisiana. She currently works as a 3rd Grade ELA Teacher for Provencal Elementary in Provencal, LA.

Meet our Spring 2021 SWDAA for Education recipient, Erik Lerma. Erik is an alumnus of the Alpha Psi chapter at West Texas A&M University. He currently works as a Jr. High and High School Band Director for Tulia ISD.

Meet our Spring 2021 SWDAA for Education recipient, Krystal Munoz. Krystal is an alumnus of the Xi Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma. She currently works as an Elementary Music Education Teacher.

Meet our Fall 2020 SWDAA for Education recipient, James Thomas. James is an alumnus of the Zeta Kappa Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi. He currently works as the Assistant band director at Carthage Middle and High School.

Meet our Fall 2019 SWDAA for Education recipient, Bo J. Galvan. Bo is an alumnus of the Alpha Psi Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi. He currently works as the Assistant Band director at Raul Perales Middle School in Laredo, Texas.

Meet one of our Fall 2018 SWDAA for Education recipients, Hannah Wells. Hanna is an alumnus of the Psi Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at the University of Arkansas. Hannah currently teaches 2nd grade education.

Meet one of our Fall 2018 SWDAA for Education recipients, Colin! Colin is an alumnus of the Tau Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma at the University of Houston and is a junior high school assistant band director.

Meet one of our Fall 2018 SWDAA for Education recipients, Matt! Matt is an alumnus of the Lambda Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi at the University of Arkansas! He currently teaches 6th - 12th grade music.
