Legends of the Southwest

This social media project that will focus on Alumni, Life, or Honorary members of the Southwest District that have shown dedication to a lifetime of service to Kappa Kappa Psi, Tau Beta Sigma, the Southwest District, and/or to College and University Bands. Spotlights will be featured on our Facebook Public Page (Southwest District Alumni Association/@SouthwestDistrictAA), our Instagram @swdalumni, and Twitter (@SWDAlumni). If you have any further questions, feel free to contact Dionicio "Dino" Cardenas at swdjointalumni@gmail.com. Form will open Fall 2021.
